About DOOM PROJECT X:

Welcome to DOOM PROJECT X, an ambitious journey into the heart of game engine development! This project is a passionate exploration fueled by the desire to understand and implement advanced rendering techniques, taking inspiration from the pioneers of the gaming industry, such as id Software's Doom and other groundbreaking titles.

At the core of DOOM PROJECT X lies a commitment to unraveling the mysteries behind 3D rendering, raycasting, and creating immersive game environments. We delve deep into the intricacies of algorithms and technologies that power some of the most iconic games in the history of the industry.

The project is driven by a community of enthusiastic developers and learners who share a common interest in pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the world of game development. Our collaborative efforts are reflected in the codebase hosted on GitHub, where you can actively participate, contribute, and learn from the wealth of knowledge within the project.

Explore our GitHub repository for DOOM PROJECT X: DOOM-PROJECT-X on GitHub.

Our exploration extends beyond traditional rendering techniques. We draw inspiration from articles, tutorials, and videos that have paved the way for understanding complex topics, such as portal rendering, intersection calculations, and the intricacies of game engine internals.

As we navigate through this fascinating landscape, we document our findings and share insights with the broader community. Stay tuned for regular updates, deep dives into specific topics, and a behind-the-scenes look at the development process.

Join us on this thrilling adventure of learning, coding, and building something extraordinary together. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting, there's a place for everyone in DOOM PROJECT X!
